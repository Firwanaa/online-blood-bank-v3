About

In today's world a number of online blood bank databases are available, however none of them offers the capability for the direct contact between the donor and the recipient. Online blood bank is an initiative to decrease the mortality rate in all the countries by providing a source to find blood easily by bring the donor to the recipient either discreetly or with a happiness on face by exchanging some hugs.
